### **SF0038 ‚Äî Ingestion Verification Protocol (IVP) v2.6**

**Canonical DOI:** https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18289047

A platform-agnostic methodological protocol defining a verifiable process
for document ingestion by AI systems.

IVP replaces implicit assumptions of ‚Äúreading‚Äù or ‚Äúunderstanding‚Äù with
explicit, observable processing requirements, including:
- Active summarization checkpoints
- External adjudication of ingestion adequacy
- Clear failure modes and prohibited shortcuts
- Explicit constraints on automation and AI‚ÄìAI delegation

### **SF0039 ‚Äî Context Representation Drift (CRD) v1.3**

**Canonical DOI:** https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18289391

A conceptual and methodological analysis identifying **Context
Representation Drift** as a distinct degradation phenomenon in extended
AI interactions.

CRD formalizes patterns often misattributed to hallucination or forgetting
and explains their impact on:
- Long-horizon reasoning
- Document ingestion reliability
- Multi-turn and delegated AI workflows

## Relationship Between the Documents

- **IVP (SF0038)** establishes verified starting conditions for document
